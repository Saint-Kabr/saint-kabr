About Us

At Gulp Roast, we're passionate about great coffee and the community it creates. Our journey began in a small garage and has grown into the coffee destination you know today.

Gulp Roast Coffee

The rich history of our company began nearly 15 years ago, when founders Dave and Amelia McGovern began roasting coffee beans in their garage. Their passion quickly grew into a thriving business, and Gulp Roast Coffee was born.

Today, we source our beans from responsible growers and roast them in small batches to ensure the freshest, most flavorful coffee possible. Our signature medium and dark roasts are available in whole bean or ground options, perfect for brewing at home or serving in your restaurant or caf√©.

"We believe that great coffee brings people together. From our small beginnings to our growing operation, that mission has remained the same." - Dave McGovern, Founder
